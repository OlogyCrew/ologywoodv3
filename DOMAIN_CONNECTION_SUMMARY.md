### If You See an Error

**Common errors and solutions:**

**Error: "DNS records not found"**
- Verify you added the records in GoDaddy correctly
- Check spelling: `104.18.26.246` and `104.18.27.246`
- Wait 5-10 minutes for DNS to propagate
- Click "Check again"

**Error: "SSL certificate provisioning failed"**
- This is rare
- Wait 5 minutes and click "Check again"
- If persists, contact Manus support

**Error: "Domain already in use"**
- The domain might be connected to another account
- Contact Manus support at https://help.manus.im
