## NOTES FOR USERS

### How to Use This Template

1. **Download & Customize:** Download this template and customize it based on your specific needs.

2. **Fill in Details:** Complete all applicable sections with specific information about the performance.

3. **Prioritize Requirements:** Mark required items with checkboxes and specify quantities/details.

4. **Review & Negotiate:** Share with the other party and negotiate any disputed terms.

5. **Sign & Store:** Once agreed upon, have both parties sign and keep copies for records.

### Tips for Artists

- **Be Specific:** The more detailed your rider, the better the venue can prepare.
- **Prioritize:** List must-haves separately from nice-to-haves.
- **Be Reasonable:** Unrealistic demands may deter venues from booking you.
- **Update Regularly:** Keep your standard rider updated as your needs change.
- **Communicate:** Discuss any special requirements early in the booking process.

### Tips for Venues

- **Read Carefully:** Review the entire rider before committing.
- **Ask Questions:** Clarify any unclear requirements with the artist.
- **Plan Ahead:** Start preparations early to meet all technical requirements.
- **Confirm Details:** Do a final confirmation call 48 hours before the event.
- **Document:** Keep records of all communications and agreements.
